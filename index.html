Memos AI - Automatic Note Taking

The ultimate note-taking app for anyone who hates writing down everything or struggles to keep up with fast-paced lectures.

With Memos AI, you can just sit back and listen while the app records, transcribes, and summarizes the key points for you.

Memos AI is more than just a voice recorder, it's a smart assistant that helps you learn better and faster.

Features

  • Record lectures, meetings, interviews etc
  • Transcribe audio to text in real-time
  • AI takes notes based on the transcript
  • Summarize key points and highlights
  • Share notes with classmates or colleagues
  • Have your notes read back to you

How it works

  1. Open the Memos AI app on your device
  2. Tap Start Recording to start recording the audio of the lecture or meeting
  3. Thats it! Memos AI will take it from here
